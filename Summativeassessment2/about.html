the Bristol Planetarium Project

Official Digital Gallery of the Big Planetarium Bristol Group

Welcome to the Planetarium. As part of our mission at the Bristol group, we are thrilled to showcase this digital experience exploring our cosmic neighborhood. This platform was developed to bring the wonders of deep space to a wider audience.

Our vision is to bridge the gap between complex astronomical research and intuitive design. This project serves as a testament to how modern tools can be used to present the beauty of the Milky Way in a clear, educational format for all stargazers.
